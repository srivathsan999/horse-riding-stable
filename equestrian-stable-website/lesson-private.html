About This Lesson

Our private lessons offer the most effective way to improve your riding skills. You will receive 100% of the instructor's attention, allowing for personalized feedback and accelerated progress.

Whether you are a complete beginner or an advanced competitive rider, we tailor the session to your goals. We focus on position, balance, and communication with the horse.

What You'll Learn

  • Correct riding posture and balance
  • Effective use of aids
  • Horse care and tacking up (optional)
  • Confidence building exercises

$85 / hour

  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Skill Level: All Levels
  • Age: 6+ Years
