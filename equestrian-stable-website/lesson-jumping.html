Jumping Clinics

Advanced Show Jumping Training

About This Clinic

Designed for intermediate to advanced riders, our Jumping Clinics focus on technical precision and course readiness. Led by former competitive show jumpers, these sessions are intense and highly rewarding.

We break down courses into manageable sections, focusing on strides, approach, and jump form. These clinics are perfect for riders preparing for competition or looking to level up their jumping skills.

What You'll Learn

  • Course memorization & analysis
  • Stride adjustment & distances
  • Improving jump form & position
  • Managing competition nerves

$120 / session

  • Duration: 90 Minutes
  • Skill Level: Intermediate+
  • Riders: Max 4 per group
