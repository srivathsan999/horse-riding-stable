Group Lessons

Social Riding Experience

About This Lesson

Our group lessons are a fantastic way to enjoy riding in a social environment. Depending on your skill level, you will be matched with riders of similar ability, making it a fun and supportive experience.

You'll learn not only from the instructor but also by observing your peers. Group lessons help improve spatial awareness, control in company, and build long-lasting friendships within the Equestrian community.

What You'll Learn

  • Riding in a squad
  • School figures and drills
  • Improved independence
  • Safe group riding etiquette

$60 / person

  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Group Size: 3-5 Riders
  • Age: 8+ Years
