Our Legacy

Decades of Equestrian Excellence

Founded in 1985, Highland Equestrian Center has been the cornerstone of the local riding community for over 35 years. What started as a small family farm has grown into one of the region's most respected training facilities.

Our commitment to the welfare of the horse and the education of the rider remains unchanged. We combine traditional horsemanship with modern training techniques to create a holistic learning environment.
