About Our Stable

Heritage, Passion, and Excellence in every detail.

Our Story

Founded in 1985

Highland Equestrian Center began as a small family stable and has grown into one of the region's premier equestrian facilities. Our philosophy is simple: the horse comes first. We believe in building a partnership based on mutual respect and understanding.

With 50 acres of pristine pasture, state-of-the-art indoor and outdoor arenas, and a team of dedicated professionals, we provide an environment where both horse and rider can thrive.
