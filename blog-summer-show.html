Upcoming Summer Show

Posted on September 28, 2026

Mark your calendars! Our annual Summer Show is returning this year with exciting new classes and competitions for riders of all levels.

Join us for a spectacular weekend of equestrian excellence, featuring show jumping, dressage, and hunter classes. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or entering your first show, we have something for everyone.

Event Details

The show will take place over two days, Saturday and Sunday, from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Our outdoor arena will host the jumping events, while dressage tests will be held in our covered arena.

Competition Classes

Beginner Division: Perfect for first-time competitors, featuring ground poles and small cross-rails up to 18 inches.

Intermediate Division: For developing riders, with courses up to 2'6" and basic dressage tests.

Advanced Division: Challenging courses up to 3'6" and FEI-level dressage tests for experienced competitors.

Registration Information

Early bird registration is now open! Register before the deadline to receive a 15% discount on entry fees. All participants will receive a commemorative t-shirt and ribbon.

Spectator Information

Admission is free for spectators! Bring your family and friends to enjoy a day of world-class equestrian sport. Food trucks and vendor booths will be available throughout the weekend.
